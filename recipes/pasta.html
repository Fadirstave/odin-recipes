Tomato and Basil Pasta

Tomato and Basil Pasta is a simple yet flavorful dish featuring cooked pasta tossed with cherry tomatoes, fresh basil leaves, minced garlic sautéed in olive oil, and seasoned with salt and pepper. It's a quick and delicious meal perfect for any occasion.

Ingredients:

  • 200g pasta
  • 2 cups cherry tomatoes, halved
  • 1/4 cup fresh basil leaves, chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 3 tbsp olive oil
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. Cook pasta according to package instructions. Drain and set aside.
  2. In a large pan, heat olive oil over medium heat. Add minced garlic and sauté until fragrant.
  3. Add cherry tomatoes to the pan and cook until they start to soften, about 3-4 minutes.
  4. Toss cooked pasta in the pan with tomatoes and garlic.
  5. Stir in chopped basil leaves, season with salt and pepper.
  6. Serve hot.
