Grilled Cheese Sandwich

Grilled Cheese Sandwich is a classic comfort food made by sandwiching cheddar cheese between two slices of buttered bread. The sandwich is then grilled until the bread turns golden brown and crispy, and the cheese melts to creamy perfection. It's a simple yet satisfying dish enjoyed warm.

Ingredients:

  • 2 slices bread
  • 2 slices cheddar cheese
  • Butter

Instructions:

  1. Heat a skillet or griddle over medium heat.
  2. Butter one side of each slice of bread.
  3. Place one slice of bread, butter-side down, on the skillet.
  4. Add cheese slices on top of the bread.
  5. Place the second slice of bread on top, butter-side up.
  6. Cook until the bottom slice of bread is golden brown and the cheese starts to melt, about 2-3 minutes.
  7. Flip the sandwich and cook until the other side is golden brown and the cheese is completely melted.
  8. Remove from heat and serve warm.
