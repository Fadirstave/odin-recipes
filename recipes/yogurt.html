Greek Yogurt Parfait

Greek Yogurt Parfait is a healthy and flavorful dish layered with Greek yogurt, granola, and mixed berries. It can be topped with honey for added sweetness, making it a perfect choice for a quick and nutritious snack or breakfast.

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup Greek yogurt
  • 1/2 cup granola
  • 1/2 cup mixed berries (strawberries, blueberries, raspberries)
  • Honey (optional)

Instructions:

  1. In a glass or bowl, layer Greek yogurt, granola, and mixed berries.
  2. Repeat layers until ingredients are used up.
  3. Drizzle honey on top if desired.
  4. Serve immediately as a healthy snack or breakfast option.
